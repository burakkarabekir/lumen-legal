Delete Your Account

Lumen · Last updated 2026-07-10

Lumen (developed by Burak Karabekir) is a private journaling app for recording moments, moods, and reflections. You can permanently delete your Lumen account and all associated data at any time using either method below.

Option 1 — Delete from within the app

  1. Open the Lumen app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm in the dialog that appears.

Your account is deleted immediately, you are signed out, and the copy of your data stored on your device is erased.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email support@lumenjournalapp.com from the address associated with your account and ask us to delete your account. We action deletion requests within 30 days.

What is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • your account data (email address, display name, and any Google or Apple account identifier and profile photo);
  • your journal content (entry text, titles, moods, tags, and any photos, voice recordings, videos, and location data you added);
  • your AI reflection data;
  • all other personal data associated with your account.

What may be retained

When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we must retain limited information to comply with legal obligations. Consent records may be retained as long as needed to evidence consent. Purchase and subscription records held by the app stores (Google Play, Apple) are governed by those stores.
